My Story

HaxelAI is a powerful tool for the FNF modding community because it makes creating and customizing mods in Psych Engine faster, easier, and more accessible for everyone. By automating repetitive tasks and helping with things like code, charting, and event setup, HaxelAI lets modders focus more on creativity and less on technical hurdles. New modders can use it to learn and experiment without feeling overwhelmed, while experienced creators can speed up their workflow and test ideas more efficiently. Overall, HaxelAI helps bring more polished, creative, and unique mods to the community, encouraging collaboration and making modding more fun for everyone involved.
